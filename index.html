Mission

Who we are

Beyond Diabetes at Stony Brook University is a student-led organization that creates an inclusive and educational community for students affected by diabetes, as well as those interested in understanding and supporting individuals living with the condition.

We believe that managing a chronic condition shouldn't mean navigating it alone. Our organization bridges the gap between medical complexity and daily student life — offering peer support, educational programming, and a resource network built specifically for the Stony Brook community.

Our Digital Infrastructure Chair publishes monthly research on advancements in diabetes technology — from continuous glucose monitors and insulin pumps to closed-loop systems and emerging non-invasive solutions — to keep our members informed about the tools that may meaningfully affect their lives.

Device & Care Guide

Find the right tools
for your care.

A curated reference for students navigating CGMs, insulin pumps, and connected pen systems — with links to manufacturer resources.

Heads up: This guide is for educational reference only. Always consult your endocrinologist or diabetes care team before changing devices or management approaches. Insurance coverage and device availability vary.

CGM Continuous Glucose Monitors
Dexcom
G7
10-day wear, 30-minute warm-up, fully disposable all-in-one sensor/transmitter. Real-time readings on iOS/Android and Apple Watch. MARD ~8.2%.
Visit site
Abbott
FreeStyle Libre 3
14-day wear, no fingerstick calibration required, 1-minute sensor warm-up. World's smallest CGM at time of launch. Real-time glucose every minute.
Visit site
Medtronic
Guardian 4
7-day wear, calibration-free, designed to integrate directly with Medtronic's MiniMed 780G pump for closed-loop automation.
Visit site
Dexcom
Stelo (OTC)
First FDA-cleared OTC CGM for adults without insulin. 15-day wear. Available without a prescription — a major step for broader diabetes monitoring access.
Visit site
Pumps Insulin Pumps & Automated Delivery
Insulet
Omnipod 5
Tubeless patch pump with SmartAdjust algorithm for automated insulin delivery. Pairs with Dexcom G6/G7. FDA-cleared hybrid closed-loop for Type 1.
Visit site
Tandem
t:slim X2 with Control-IQ
Tubed pump with predictive low glucose suspend and automated correction boluses. Pairs with Dexcom G6 or G7. Remotely updatable via software.
Visit site
Medtronic
MiniMed 780G
Advanced hybrid closed-loop with auto-correction boluses every 5 minutes. Pairs with Guardian 4 CGM. Targets a glucose level set by the user.
Visit site
Smart Pens Connected Insulin Pens
Novo Nordisk
NovoPen 6
Reusable connected insulin pen that automatically records dose time and amount. Data syncs to diabetes management apps. Works with Dexcom CGM data.
Visit site
Medtronic
InPen
Smart reusable pen with bolus calculator app. Tracks doses, temperature, and timing. Integrates with Dexcom and Abbott CGM data for recommended dosing support.
Visit site
